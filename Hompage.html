Neon Genesis Evangelion is a Japanese anime series written and directed by Hideaki Anno and First Broadcasted in 1995 at Tv Tokyo.

The Series has 1 season with 26 Episodes and 1 movie, but also 5 Rebuild Movies and a Godzilla vs Evangelion movie (we dont talk about that one...) that aired between 1995 and 1996.

On the Surface, it is a action packed mecha anime, but soon after starting the show, you realise that it is way deeper than that.

The story follows a 14 year old boy, Shinji Ikari, who is forced to pilot a Giant humanoid Robot Called Evangelion unit 01, in short EVA-01, to fight the incomming waves of enemys that are called Angels.
